Using NFL game stats recorded from all games played between the 2002 and 2019 season, this tutorial will analyze the various factors (both offensive and defensive) that contribute to the outcome of a game, and determine the most important factors that predict the outcome a game. Once these factors have been determined, we will then use those statistics to try and predict the winner of two teams, based on each team's stats. For NFL fans, we hope to provide some interesting insights that they may have not known, as well as some more intuition on how to determine if a team will win. This tutorial simply caluclates scoring and win predictions, and is not responsible for any ill placed bets or money lost.

Data Manipulation¶

From the first five entires in the dataset displayed above, all of the statistics are reported for both home and away teams. To begin, the differences between each away and home statistics is calculated and added as a new column to the dataframe. By subtracting the two values, we can see the net difference between the away vs home team to get an initial estimate if the location of the game has an impact. The first row of the updated dataframe is displayed to show the new additions to the dataset.

By graphing the scatterplot, we can compare the average passing yards and rushing yards of each team to get a better understanding of which offensive method each team utilized more. Based on the chart, we can see that a couple of teams stand out for having high passing yards: Green Bay Packers, Indiannapolis Colds, New England Patriots, New Orleans Saints. In comparison, a lot more teams have high rushing yards than they do passing yards, as the top left corner is more dense than the bottom right. The teams with the highest rushing yards are Minnesota Vikings, Denver Broncos, Kansas City Chiefs, Seattle Seahawkws, Carolina Panthers. It appears that in general, more teams tend to rush than to pass, and not many teams have many yards in both rushing and passing. The team with the best average passing and rushing yards is the New England patriots, but their rushing yards is nowhere near the top rushing teams.
